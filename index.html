Is AI Destroying the Internet?

October 18, 2024 by Connor H.

I often wonder what’s real (in the sense that it was created by a human) and what isn’t when it comes to internet content in the modern world. With AI content being so numerous nowadays, it’s hard to pinpoint what exactly is being truthful, or what is garbled and or outright incorrect garbage. Typically when I'm surfing through social media, watching short form videos, reading small condensed articles, and gazing at eye capturing pictures, I often see a handful of these pieces of media with the same, or at the very least similarly styled, AI generated voices, scripts, and pictures, which really makes me wonder if what I am watching is truly “real” as well as making me question the validity of what people or articles online are saying.

The Growing Problem

Artificial Intelligence, while very useful, can also make many mistakes. In a lot of cases, it can show totally irrelevant information for what you are trying to read about. This can lead to a lot of misinformation and total trash on the internet as AI is unable to tell what is real and or relevant and what is not. For example: (see image below)

How Can We Combat This?

In truth, there isn't really a way for us to outright stop this kind of content from resurfacing every so often. However there are a few tricks to keep an open mind to AI content so you aren’t fooled by it either.
